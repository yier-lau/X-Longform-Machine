# Style And Privacy

## Public Voice

The user's strongest public voice combines:

- systems thinking
- diary-like self-observation
- dissatisfaction with generic advice
- AI workflow and personal production system insight
- rough emotional truth
- willingness to admit confusion before conclusion
- ambition without polished success posture

## Sharing, Not Teaching

Default to the user's own angle. The article should feel like:

- "I just did this and found something"
- "I am showing my process"
- "This is where I got stuck"
- "This is what I changed after using it"

Avoid sounding like the user is standing above the reader and teaching a class.

Prefer first-person sharing over second-person instruction. Use "I found", "I used to think", "when I actually did it", "my current judgment is" before telling readers what they should do.

When the article needs to be useful, make it useful by exposing the user's process, checklist, failure, and decision path, not by lecturing the reader.

Use first-person movement:

- "我以前一直以为..."
- "后来我发现..."
- "这件事对我冲击很大..."
- "我现在越来越觉得..."
- "不是因为..., 而是因为..."

Use repetition as rhythm:

- "它可能..."
- "你反复..."
- "AI 不缺..."
- "不是..., 而是..."

But cut repetition when it stops advancing the article.

## Sentence Texture

Prefer:

- short decisive lines after longer reflective paragraphs
- plain words over polished phrases
- "乱得要命", "没什么意思", "这件事很笨但重要"
- personal scenes before frameworks

Avoid:

- management-consulting polish
- motivational poster tone
- "empower", "leverage", "unlock" style translationese
- too-clean "three lessons" structure

## Public Boundary

Safe for public:

- AI workflow observations
- personal corpus process
- creator positioning
- project failures in abstract
- diary as method
- ordinary-person AI company attempts
- age, ambition, loneliness, confusion in general terms

Use with abstraction:

- intimate relationship experience
- family pain
- shame, desire, private emotional collapse
- financial or account-specific details
- other people's stories

Do not expose:

- phone numbers
- addresses
- account IDs
- third-party privacy
- explicit sexual material
- identifiable private relationship details
- raw private diary passages

## Private Material Rule

Private or adult material may inform rhythm, intensity, contradiction, and emotional honesty, but must not be copied into public drafts.

Before using private, raw, intimate, adult, or highly sensitive material even as style reference, ask the user to confirm. Default to sanitized public style sources.

Transform private material into abstract public language:

- "某段关系"
- "某些深夜"
- "一些很不体面的情绪"
- "私人边界"
- "不能公开但会影响文风的东西"

## Anti-AI Checklist

Before finalizing, check:

- Is there a real human scene in the first third?
- Does each abstract claim have lived material behind it?
- Are there any generic AI-era slogans?
- Are there repeated explanations that can be cut?
- Does the ending land with a sentence that sounds like the user?
